Occupational Health Services

Comprehensive workplace health solutions and immigration medical services.

Partnering with Businesses for Employee Health

We provide comprehensive occupational health services to ensure a safe, healthy, and compliant workforce. From pre-employment screenings to injury treatment, we're your trusted healthcare partner.

Pre-Employment Physicals

Comprehensive medical examinations to ensure candidates are fit for their intended roles.

Immigration Medical Exams

USCIS-authorized civil surgeon providing required medical examinations for immigration purposes.

Work Injury Treatment

Prompt evaluation and treatment of workplace injuries with focus on quick recovery and return to work.

Drug & Alcohol Testing

DOT and non-DOT testing services with quick turnaround times and accurate results.

Fitness for Duty Exams

Assessments to determine if employees can safely perform their job functions after illness or injury.

Employee Wellness Programs

Customized wellness programs to promote employee health and reduce healthcare costs.
